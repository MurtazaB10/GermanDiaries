About Me

My name is Apurva. I have studied German literature at Faculty of arts, Delhi university. I have also learnt the language at the Goethe Institute, New Delhi till level C1. I love the language and its literature. I intent to make learning German language fun for its learners and there is nothing more interesting than solving a puzzle. It will not only increase your vocabulary, which is very important but also will be a good way to spend your free time. Learning through stories is another good way to learn a foreign language. So I have translated Panchatantra stories into German. Panchatantra stories have been an integral part of the childhood of almost every Indian. The purpose of translating those stories into German was to make learners read and get a grip on the language and enjoy reading the tiny tales with a big moral.
